FAQ

How do I link a senior's account? expand_more

Go to Settings > Link Account and enter the unique pairing code generated on the senior's device.

How does Scam Shield work? expand_more

Our AI analyzes incoming calls and messages in real-time to detect common scam patterns and warns the user or guardian immediately.

Can I set multiple geofences? expand_more

Yes, you can create multiple "Safe Zones" around locations like home, the park, or a doctor's office.

How accurate is the SOS fall detection? expand_more

Our system uses advanced motion sensors and machine learning to detect hard falls with up to 95% accuracy, instantly notifying guardians.

Can I export the health reports for a doctor? expand_more

Absolutely. Navigate to the Health section and tap the "Export" button at the top right to download a secure PDF to share with medical professionals.

What if the senior's phone dies? expand_more

You will receive a low battery alert when their device hits 15%, and a final location ping right before the device powers off.

How do I add or change a medicine reminder? expand_more

Open Medicine, tap the add button, and enter the medicine name, dosage, date, and time. Tap an existing reminder to update it.

How do I book a doctor appointment? expand_more

Open Doctor Appointments, choose a doctor, select an available visit type, date, and time, then confirm the booking.

Can I reschedule or cancel an appointment? expand_more

Yes. Open Upcoming Appointments, select the booking, and choose reschedule or cancel. Availability and cancellation rules may depend on the clinic.

What can CareMate Assistant help me with? expand_more

CareMate Assistant can answer general wellness questions, explain app features, and help you find reminders and health information. It does not replace professional medical advice.

Why is voice assistance not hearing me? expand_more

Check that microphone permission is enabled for CareMate, move to a quieter place, and try again. You can always use the keyboard instead.

How do I make my account private? expand_more

Go to Settings > Privacy and enable Privacy Mode. Only people you approve will be able to view your shared content.

Who can see my health information? expand_more

Your private health information is visible only to you and approved guardians or care providers. Public community posts do not include private health records.

How do I block or report another user? expand_more

Open the user's profile, tap the options menu, and choose Block or Report. Blocked accounts can be managed later in Privacy settings.

How do I change or reset my password? expand_more

Open Settings > Change Password. If you cannot sign in, use Forgot Password on the login screen and follow the verification steps.

Does SOS work without an internet connection? expand_more

Some emergency actions may use your phone's calling or SMS service, but live location and app notifications require a network connection.
