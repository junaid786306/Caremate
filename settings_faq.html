FAQ

How do I link a senior's account? expand_more

Go to Settings > Link Account and enter the unique pairing code generated on the senior's device.

How does Scam Shield work? expand_more

Our AI analyzes incoming calls and messages in real-time to detect common scam patterns and warns the user or guardian immediately.

Can I set multiple geofences? expand_more

Yes, you can create multiple "Safe Zones" around locations like home, the park, or a doctor's office.

How accurate is the SOS fall detection? expand_more

Our system uses advanced motion sensors and machine learning to detect hard falls with up to 95% accuracy, instantly notifying guardians.

Can I export the health reports for a doctor? expand_more

Absolutely. Navigate to the Health section and tap the "Export" button at the top right to download a secure PDF to share with medical professionals.

What if the senior's phone dies? expand_more

You will receive a low battery alert when their device hits 15%, and a final location ping right before the device powers off.
