Privacy Policy

1. Information We Collect

Caremate collects health data (steps, medication schedules, vitals) and personal information (name, email, age) to provide tailored wellness support for senior users.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used to generate health summaries, send reminders, and facilitate community interactions. We never sell your data to third parties.

3. Data Security

We implement military-grade encryption to ensure your medical records and personal details are always protected.
